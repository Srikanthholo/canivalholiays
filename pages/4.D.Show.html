4.D.Show(Worli)

4.D.Show(Worli)


Orama 4D Theatre: Feel the 4D action in a film at Orama, Atria Mall. This theatre claims to be the only one of its kind in the world to have 63 4D, 9 stimulators and chairs with 4D effects and is a complete sensory experience. The 4D movies are specially formatted and of 7 20 minutes duration. The 70 80 seater theatre is designed to provide a 3-dimensional effect. You can expect special features like leg ticklers, air blasts, vibrators, water blasts, vibrating seats, foam, fog, hot and cold air blasts, fragrances and scent, wind, snow, laser light effects and multi channel audio sounds.
