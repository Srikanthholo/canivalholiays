Bandra Reclimation

Bandra Reclimation


Beautiful Bandra Reclamation is at Suburb location of Mumbai and is a very neatly developed and maintained. Reclamation is a very special spot for hangout sightseeing with nice cool breeze ta sea side. It has nice sitting arrangements and of course the roof is always there to seat if the seats are full. With beautiful flowers and plants around one surely will like to spend some quality time with there beloveds and families here at Bandra. Reclamation has lovely sunset spot and a nice view towards developing Bandra Worli Sea link project.

From beautiful flyover of reclamation one can enjoy complete sea view (See Picture Below). Famous places near Bandra Reclamation are Taj hotel, Lilavati Hospital one can see by standing on the bridge. College groups and Couples are always to be seen for this kind of romantic place and a lovers point of Mumbai with less disturbance.

Monsoon at Reclamation is to be seen and enjoyed with flowing water hitting the sea rocks at the shore jumping over you, where you can sit. Parking at Reclamation flyovers and sea shore front is not allowed.
