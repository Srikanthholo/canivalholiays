Mr. George founded Carnival Holidays in 2000 with the objective of providing the Mumbaikars with SUPERIOR quality package tour amenities and to offer Sightseeing, locally known as Mumbai Darshan. Carnival Holidays is the largest provider of city bus transportation in Mumbai, serving about 500 people each day and offering around two schedules per day. Carnival Holidays is well-known for its reliability and on-time performance, as well as for providing consumers with value-added services. The company's tour service provides economical tour packages for businesses, schools, and other organisations.
